We love to perform-
no matter the occasion

Whether it’s performing on TV, entertaining for community events, or getting dressed up for your formal occasion, we bring award-winning performances wherever we go.

We’re always on the lookout for talented,
fun-loving voices and personalities!

Our members come from all walks of campus life and
represent a diverse community of UC San Diego.
We encourage everyone with a passion for singing to
audition— regardless of your formal vocal background.
